DESIGNER YOUNG FASHION HOUSE

Celebrating Liberian Heritage Through Fashion

DESIGNER YOUNG FASHION HOUSE was founded in 2025 with a vision to bring Liberian fashion to the global stage. We combine traditional Liberian & African textile artistry with contemporary design to create unique, culturally-rich fashion pieces.

Our designs are inspired by the diverse cultures across the African continent, from the vibrant Wrapper prints of West Africa to the intricate beadwork of East Africa and the earthy tones of Southern African textiles.

We work directly with All Liberian Design Collaboration to ensure authentic craftsmanship while providing fair wages and supporting local communities. Each piece in our collection tells a story of heritage, creativity, and modern elegance.

Our mission is to empower individuals to express their identity through fashion that honors tradition while embracing contemporary style.

All Liberian Design Collaboration

We partner directly with All Liberian Fashion Design to preserve traditional crafting techniques.

Sustainable Materials

Our fabrics are ethically sourced and we prioritize eco-friendly production methods.

Award-Winning Designs

Our collections have received national recognition for innovation in Liberian fashion.
